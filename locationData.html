Crisis Alerted

This App tracks the location of the users, and lets the add geolocation pins on points of interest. This can be anything from major disasters to minor inconveniences. This allows all users to have a greater awareness about the town around them, and allows them to avoid things such as car crashes and traffic jams. This is applicable for large scale cities as well as smaller scale campuses. This app is by students, for students.
